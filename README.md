# Starlet 5.0 

![Starlet Logo](public/brand/Logo.png)

**Starlet** is the ultimate innovation marathon for women where ideas meet execution and strangers become teammates. Whether you're a seasoned coder or just stepping into the world of tech, Starlet is your stage to shine, build, and grow.

### A Mind Empowered Initiative 
Starlet is a flagship event by **Mind Empowered**, dedicated to eliminating the stigma associated with mental health and empowering students through innovation and community.
